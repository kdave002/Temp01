# Design Partner Program — One Pager

## Problem Statement
Growth-stage teams struggle to turn AI prototypes into reliable, ROI-positive workflows. Most pilots stall due to unclear scope, weak adoption, and no measurable business outcomes.

## Who It’s For
- Ops, RevOps, Support, and Product leaders at SMB/mid-market companies
- Teams with repetitive, manual workflows and clear efficiency bottlenecks
- Decision-makers who want fast validation before broader AI rollout

## Pilot Scope (6 Weeks)
- 1 high-value workflow selected jointly (e.g., support triage, lead qualification, internal knowledge retrieval)
- Discovery + baseline metrics (Week 1)
- Build + integrations + lightweight change management (Weeks 2–4)
- Live pilot with weekly optimization (Weeks 5–6)
- End-of-pilot executive readout and scale recommendation

## Success Metrics
- **Efficiency:** 25–40% reduction in cycle time for target workflow
- **Quality:** 15–25% reduction in errors/escalations
- **Adoption:** 70%+ active usage by target team during pilot
- **Business impact:** Clear ROI model with payback path under 6 months

## Timeline
- **Week 0:** Partner alignment + legal/procurement
- **Week 1:** Discovery, process mapping, KPI baseline
- **Weeks 2–4:** Solution build, integration, testing, enablement
- **Weeks 5–6:** Production pilot, optimization, final readout

## What We Need From the Customer
- Executive sponsor and day-to-day owner
- Access to SMEs (2–4 hrs/week)
- Access to relevant systems/data (sandbox or controlled production)
- Agreement on baseline metrics and decision criteria
- Fast feedback cadence (weekly 30-minute working session)

## Pricing Hypothesis
- **Pilot Fee:** $20K–$45K fixed (depends on integration complexity and data readiness)
- **Conversion Path:** If pilot KPIs are met, transition to annual program ($4K–$12K/month) with pilot fee credited 25–50% toward year-one subscription
- **Goal:** De-risk adoption while creating a clear path to scaled rollout
